Welcome to Paintball Pro

Paintball Pro is a top-down cooperative shooter inspired by the real-world game Paintball. There is both a Single Player (4v4 with AI) and Multiplayer (4v4 with real players/AI) version of the game, with two different game modes – Team Match and Capture the Flag.

Team Match

In Team Match, players are separated into two teams – Red and Blue, of equal numbers. Teams attempt to shoot players on the opposing team to earn points for their own team. At the end of the 3-minute game, the team with the highest overall score wins.

Capture the Flag

In Capture the Flag, teams are once again split into Red and Blue teams of equal members, and players attempt to take control of a flag – returning it to their spawn base to earn points for their team. The team with the highest number of points at the end of the 3-minute game wins.
